Focaccia with caramelized onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holliday starter!

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons in istant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

For the toppings:

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegrar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes
  • 11/2cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

    Make the focaccia dough

in a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough Ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the doug, though I sometimes kead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
